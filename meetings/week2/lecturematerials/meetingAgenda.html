Typical Meeting Agenda

  • Individual project to work on right when you arrive - 5 minutes
  • Greeting and updates - 5 minutes
  • Review of Project - 10 minutes
  • Exposition and teaching about the topic for the meeting - 15 minutes
  • Quick overview of successful website or design concept - 10 minutes
  • Collaboration between people in the club during the club meeting on tasks - 20 minutes
  • Review of in club projects and vote - 10 minutes
  • Wrap up, survey, and agenda for next week - 2 minutes
