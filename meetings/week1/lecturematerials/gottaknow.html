Some things you just have to know

HTML

  • Doctype - not a tag but you need to be familiar with it
  • html - body and head are its only children elements
  • head - holds most of the information needed to render your page and talk to google
  • body - holds all of your content
  • script tag, link tag, and charset
  • id and class attributes for all elements
  • headers, paragraphs, divs, and unordered lists
  • anchors, images, and their attributes

CSS

  • Selectors and resets
  • Positioning and Display
  • Width, Height, Min and Max
  • Background, Color, and Hash colors
