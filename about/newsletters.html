Week 1 Newsletter

Dear Web Development Club Members! Welcome to the first club newsletter of Spring 2014! I hope you were able to register for your complementary unit as part of being in the club this semester. The first meeting on Monday was a success, but we didn't get a large turnout on Thursdays (1-230)so make sure to let people who can't make the Monday meeting know about the Thursday session. Separately, I apologize for the speed that the material was covered in Mondays club meeting, we will be reviewing it further next Monday to help you get more practice. I'd recommend that you learn a little about CSS Positioning by reading this CSS Article. Also, the files from week 1 are now up on Google Drive Here! View the readme first! Also the site has received a large update so if you want to check out the tutorials area you might find some useful links and tips. Of course it is still under construction, but if you want to help or have ideas to improve it feel free to email me. Thanks again everyone, and I hope I see you all next week!

Sincerely, Erik Mellum
